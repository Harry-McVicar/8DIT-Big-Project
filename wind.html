Wind Power

Wind power is based on the concept of aerodynamics as the wind turbines must be designed to optimise rotation due to movement in the air acting in any direction. This follows the concept of energy transformation as the kenetic energy from the molecules in the atomsophere and hence wind turbine must be converted into electrical energy which can be stored and used as needed. This is extremely important and fundamental to the production of energy for both domestic and commercial use.
